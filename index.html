Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QariAI

How does QariAI analyze my recitation?

QariAI uses advanced AI speech recognition trained on Quranic recitation. It analyzes your pronunciation at the phoneme level, checking articulation points (Makharij), timing (Ghunnah, Madd), and letter characteristics (Sifaat). The AI compares your recitation against expert Qaris to provide precise feedback.

Is QariAI suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! QariAI has a dedicated Beginner level that focuses on foundational skills like proper letter pronunciation and basic vowel sounds. The AI provides gentle feedback and targeted drills to build your confidence gradually. Whether you're learning to read Quran for the first time or returning after years, QariAI adapts to your level.

Can I use QariAI for Hifz (memorization)?

Yes! QariAI includes a dedicated Hifz Mode with spaced repetition tracking. The AI checks both your memorization accuracy and Tajweed simultaneously. You'll get reminders for due revisions and detailed analytics on your retention strength. Perfect for students memorizing new verses or maintaining existing Hifz.

Does QariAI work offline?

QariAI works primarily online for the most accurate AI analysis. However, you can record recitations offline and they'll be analyzed once you're back online. Your progress data, Hifz lists, and analytics are stored locally and sync when connected. We're working on enhanced offline capabilities for future updates.

Is my audio data private and secure?

Yes! Your privacy is our priority. Audio recordings are processed solely for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers unless you explicitly save a session. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never use your data to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How much does QariAI cost?

QariAI is currently in free beta! Beta users get full access to all features at no cost. Once we launch publicly, we'll offer a freemium model with generous free tier (10 sessions/month) and a Pro subscription ($9.99/month) for unlimited access and advanced features. Beta users will receive special early-bird pricing.

Can QariAI replace a human Tajweed teacher?

QariAI is a powerful supplement, not a replacement for a qualified teacher. While our AI provides instant, detailed feedback on technical aspects of Tajweed, learning from a human teacher provides spiritual guidance, context, and personalized mentorship that technology cannot replicate. We recommend using QariAI for daily practice between lessons with your teacher.
