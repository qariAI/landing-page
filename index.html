Beginner Level

"Building the Foundation"

Score: 0–70%Focus: MakharijMicro-Drills

👤 Who is this for?

Those learning to read the Qur'an for the first time, returning after a long pause, or struggling with correct pronunciation — if you confuse Seen vs Saad, or feel unsure about vowel lengths, this is your starting line.

🎯 Key Focus: Major Errors (Lahm Jali)

  • →Makharij: Correct articulation from throat, tongue, or lips
  • →Harakat: Distinguishing Fatha, Kasra, and Damma clearly
  • →Basic Madd: Respecting natural elongation (2 counts)

✨ Your Experience

  • →Micro-Drills: Single letters and short words to build muscle memory
  • →Gentle Feedback: AI focuses on letter accuracy, not timing nuances
  • →Daily Streaks: Consistency is your main goal here

⬆️ Level Up Requirement

To graduate to Intermediate:

Tajweed Score 70%+

Consistently on basic verses

🕌 Spiritual Reminder: "Verily, the one who recites the Qur'an beautifully, smoothly, and precisely, he will be in the company of the noble and obedient angels." (Bukhari)

🌿

Intermediate Level

"The Art of Refinement"

Score: 70–85%Focus: Timing & RulesRegenerate Drills

👤 Who is this for?

The reciter who reads fluently without stuttering but wants to apply Tajweed rules correctly. You know the letters, but your Ghunnah may be too short or your Qalqalah missing.

🎯 Key Focus: Minor Errors (Lahm Khafi)

  • →Timing & Rhythm: Perfecting Ghunnah and Madd duration
  • →Noon & Meem Rules: Ikhfa, Idgham, Iqlab
  • →Qalqalah: Correct "echo" on the five bouncing letters

✨ Your Experience

  • →Complex Drills: Rule combinations via "Regenerate Drills"
  • →Visual Feedback: Radar chart shows Timing and Flow balance
  • →Streak Multipliers: Daily streaks unlock XP bonuses

⬆️ Level Up Requirement

To reach Advanced:

Average Score 85%+

Maintained over 7 consecutive days

🕌 Spiritual Reminder: "Beautify the Qur'an with your voices, for indeed the beautiful voice increases the Qur'an in beauty." (Sunan al-Darimi)

🌳

Advanced Level

"Mastery & Ihsan"

Score: 85–100%Focus: SifaatElite Drills + Hifz

👤 Who is this for?

Aspiring Huffaz, teachers, or those seeking perfection (Ihsan). You are refining every breath and sound, aiming to recite with the precision of the great Qaris.

🎯 Key Focus: Sifaat (Letter Characteristics)

  • →Sifaat: Hams, Istila, Safir
  • →Tafkheem & Tarqeeq: Heavy/light quality of Ra and Lam
  • →Waqf & Ibtida: Where to stop and resume correctly
  • →Breath Control: Long verses without gasping

✨ Your Experience

  • →Elite Drills: Heavy/light letter transition challenges
  • →Full Hifz Mode: Memory accuracy tested alongside Tajweed
  • →Granular Analytics: Exactly which letter prevents 100%

Score 95%+ consistently

A drop prompts AI to suggest Intermediate review drills

🕌 Spiritual Reminder: "Read and elevate through the levels of Paradise and recite as you used to recite in the world, for indeed your position is at the last verse you recite." (Abu Dawood)

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QariAI

How does QariAI analyze my recitation?

QariAI uses advanced AI speech recognition trained on Quranic recitation. It analyzes your pronunciation at the phoneme level, checking articulation points (Makharij), timing (Ghunnah, Madd), and letter characteristics (Sifaat). The AI compares your recitation against expert Qaris to provide precise feedback.

Is QariAI suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! QariAI has a dedicated Beginner level that focuses on foundational skills like proper letter pronunciation and basic vowel sounds. The AI provides gentle feedback and targeted drills to build your confidence gradually.

Can I use QariAI for Hifz (memorization)?

Yes! QariAI includes a dedicated Hifz Mode with spaced repetition tracking. The AI checks both your memorization accuracy and Tajweed simultaneously. You'll get reminders for due revisions and detailed analytics on your retention strength.

Does QariAI work offline?

QariAI works primarily online for the most accurate AI analysis. However, you can record recitations offline and they'll be analyzed once you're back online. Your progress data, Hifz lists, and analytics are stored locally and sync when connected.

Is my audio data private and secure?

Yes! Your privacy is our priority. Audio recordings are processed solely for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers unless you explicitly save a session. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never use your data to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How much does QariAI cost?

QariAI is currently in free beta! Beta users get full access to all features at no cost. Once we launch publicly, we'll offer a freemium model with a free tier (5 analyses/day) and a Pro subscription (£4.99/month) for unlimited access plus Hifz tracking. Beta users will receive special early-bird pricing.

Can QariAI replace a human Tajweed teacher?

QariAI is a powerful supplement, not a replacement for a qualified teacher. While our AI provides instant, detailed feedback on technical aspects of Tajweed, learning from a human teacher provides spiritual guidance, context, and personalized mentorship that technology cannot replicate.
