How it works

Three simple steps

No signups. No fees. Just you, a sūrah, and a masjid a day.

1

Download QariAI

Install free on Google Play. Open the app and pick your starting masjid — ten sacred environments, from Al-Masjid an-Nabawi to Süleymaniye.

2

Recite daily for 10 days

Each day, tap the mic and recite a sūrah or passage. The AI listens to your Tajweed in real-time and tells you exactly what to fix.

On day 10, your shareable completion card unlocks. Post it with #10MasjidsChallenge — tag a friend to join the next round.

The mechanic

Simple rules

Keep it light. This is a habit-builder, not a test.

1
One masjid a day, any sūrah. Start with Al-Fātiḥah, finish with a favorite. You choose the order.
2
Use QariAI's mic. The AI scores your Tajweed — aim for a personal best, not perfection.
3
Missed a day? Pick back up. No streak guilt — just finish all 10 within any 2-week window.
4
Share your day-10 card. Post it on Instagram/TikTok/X with #10MasjidsChallenge — tag one friend to join.
