Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026  ·  Effective: February 26, 2026

Our commitment to you: QariAI is built with the Ummah in mind. We collect only what is necessary to make your Quran learning experience exceptional, we never sell your data, and we treat your information with the same care and trust you place in us. بارك الله فيكم

📋 1. Who We Are

QariAI ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered Tajweed and Hifz coaching application developed to help Muslims worldwide improve their Quran recitation. Our service is available at qariai.app and associated mobile applications.

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at [email protected].

📦 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use QariAI:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Audio recordings: Voice recordings of your Quran recitation, submitted to our AI for Tajweed analysis. These are processed in real-time and not stored permanently unless you explicitly save a session.
  • Usage data: Which Surahs you practise, your scores, streak data, and learning progress to personalise your coaching experience.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and browser type for compatibility and performance optimisation.
  • Beta sign-up data: Your email address and any optional information you provide when joining the beta waitlist.
  • Communications: Any messages you send us via email or in-app support.

We do not collect payment information directly — this is handled by our trusted third-party payment processors.

⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve QariAI's services:

  • To analyse your recitation and provide real-time Tajweed feedback via our AI engine.
  • To track your Hifz progress, streaks, and XP level so your experience is personalised.
  • To send you important service updates, beta access notifications, and — if you opt in — learning tips and reminders.
  • To improve our AI models and app experience using aggregated, anonymised data.
  • To provide customer support when you reach out to us.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.
🚫 We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is yours.

🎙️ 4. Audio Data & Quran Recitations

We understand that your recitation of the Quran is sacred and personal. Audio submitted to QariAI is:

  • Processed by our AI to generate real-time Tajweed analysis and feedback.
  • Stored only for the duration of your session unless you choose to save a recording.
  • Never used for advertising or shared with any third party for commercial purposes.
  • Potentially used — in anonymised, aggregated form only — to improve the accuracy of our AI model, subject to your consent settings.

You may opt out of AI model improvement contributions at any time in your account settings.

🤝 5. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of carefully selected third-party services to operate QariAI:

  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure. Your data may transit through Vercel's servers.
  • Web3Forms — for beta sign-up form submissions. Only your email and name are transmitted.
  • Analytics providers — we use privacy-respecting analytics to understand app usage (no personally identifiable tracking).

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We select partners who align with our commitment to privacy.

🍪 6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage strictly for:

  • Keeping you logged in across sessions (authentication token).
  • Remembering your preferences (e.g. dark mode, selected Surah).
  • Essential analytics to understand app performance.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

🛡️ 7. Data Security

We take reasonable and industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest.
  • Access controls limiting who within our team can view personal data.
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us immediately at [email protected].

👤 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

👶 9. Children's Privacy

QariAI is designed for users of all ages, including children learning the Quran. For users under 13 years of age, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal data. Parents or guardians may contact us at [email protected] to review, update, or delete their child's data.

🌍 10. International Users

QariAI is accessible globally. If you are located outside of the country where our servers are hosted, your information may be transferred internationally. By using our service, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
