About This Planner

Quran memorisation is easy to build and easy to lose. The biggest practical challenge in Hifz isn't memorising new material — it's maintaining what you've already memorised through consistent, structured revision (murājaʿah).

This planner uses a simple fixed-interval system rather than a complex spaced-repetition algorithm — easy to understand, easy to stick to, and effective for most students. Here's the schedule logic:

Confidence LevelRevision IntervalBest For
NewEvery 1 dayJust memorised, still fragile
BuildingEvery 3 daysA few weeks of practice in
SolidEvery 7 daysConfident, long-term maintenance

As you revise a portion and mark it done, you can manually move it up a confidence level when it starts to feel solid — giving you control over your own schedule rather than a rigid algorithm dictating it.

Your data is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. It is not sent to any server and is not shared. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, your schedule will reset — there's no account system, by design, to keep this tool completely free and frictionless.
