QariAI is an AI Quranic recitation tool that listens to you recite, identifies phonetic errors and Tajweed rule violations, and gives actionable coaching feedback. It covers 16 Tajweed rules including Madd, Ghunnah, Ikhfa, Idgham, Qalqalah, and more.

Unlike static flashcard or memorization apps, QariAI analyzes audio in real time and tells you specifically which rule you missed and why — like a knowledgeable teacher in your pocket.
