AI Quran recitation coach with Tajweed rule feedback

**Description:**
QariAI listens to you recite Quran and tells you exactly which Tajweed rules you're missing — Ghunnah, Madd, Ikhfa, Qalqalah, and 12 more.

Most Quran apps help you memorize. QariAI helps you recite correctly. After each session, you get:
- A score with mistake breakdown by rule
- Plain-English explanations of what went wrong
- Word-by-word reference audio from a certified reciter
- Pattern detection that flags rules you keep missing across sessions

Built on an open evaluation framework published under CC BY 4.0 — the first standardized methodology for benchmarking AI Quran recitation tools.
