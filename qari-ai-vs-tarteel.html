Makharij Error Classification

Makharij (articulation points) are the foundation of correct Tajweed. Qari AI explicitly models where each letter originates and detects when pronunciation deviates from the correct point.

Letters Qari AI Classifies
  • Throat letters — ح، ع، هـ، غ، خ
  • Tongue letters — ل، ن، ر، ت، د
  • Lip letters — ب، م، ف، و
Error Types Detected
  • Substitution errors (e.g. ع vs أ)
  • Weak articulation
  • Incorrect Tafkheem / Tarqeeq
  • Madd length violations

Qari AI teaches why a mistake happens — not just that it happened. This is what separates a Tajweed coach from a recitation tracker.
