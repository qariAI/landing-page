About QariAI

Built with sincerity, for every Muslim who wants to recite better

🎯 Our Mission

QariAI exists for one purpose: to help every Muslim recite the Quran the way it deserves to be recited.

We believe that access to high-quality Tajweed coaching shouldn't be limited by geography, finances, or time. Whether you're in London or Lahore, a student or a parent, a beginner or someone returning after years — you deserve precise, patient, and personalised feedback on your recitation.

وَرَتِّلِ الْقُرْآنَ تَرْتِيلًا
"And recite the Quran with measured recitation." — Surah Al-Muzzammil (73:4)

That ayah is at the heart of why QariAI exists. Allah commands us to recite with care and precision — and we want to make that easier for every Muslim, one recitation at a time.

💡 Why We Built QariAI

The problem is real and it's widespread. Millions of Muslims recite the Quran daily with Tajweed mistakes they're not even aware of — not out of carelessness, but because they've never had access to consistent, expert feedback.

Traditional Tajweed learning requires finding a qualified teacher, scheduling regular sessions, and often paying fees that not everyone can afford. For many Muslims around the world, that's simply not possible.

We asked: what if AI could listen to your recitation the way a knowledgeable teacher would — catching mistakes in Makharij, Ghunnah, Madd, and Qalqalah — and give you instant, clear feedback, available anytime, anywhere, for free?

That's QariAI. An AI coach that never tires, never judges, and is always there — whether it's 3am after Tahajjud or 5 minutes before Fajr. Built not to replace teachers, but to complement them and fill the gaps where access is limited.
