What we check

Our checker identifies the most common Tajweed mistakes made by learners at every level.

🗣️
Makharij al-Huruf
Correct articulation points for each letter — throat, tongue, lips and nose.
〰️
Ghunnah
Nasalisation on Noon and Meem — duration, quality and consistency.
⏱️
Madd (Elongation)
Correct length of vowel elongations — natural, connected and obligatory Madd.

Qalqalah
The "echoing" bounce on the five Qalqalah letters at sukoon.

Ikhfa
Partial hiding of Noon Sakinah and Tanween before 15 specific letters.

Idgham
Merging of Noon Sakinah or Tanween into the following letter.
